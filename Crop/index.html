What we do

Two things we do brilliantly, and a network for everything after.

Most brand problems are really two problems wearing one coat. Work out what you stand for, then make people feel it. We do both, under one roof.

01

Brand strategy

The thinking. We get to the single organising idea that makes sense of your whole business, then turn it into a story everyone can build behind, inside and out.

02

Brand identity

The craft. We give the idea a face: a visual world, a voice and a system that works everywhere, from the logo to the last touchpoint.

03

Whatever it needs next

The reach. Website build, PR, performance, film, packaging. We bring in trusted specialists from our network and run it, so the brand arrives fully formed.

Why Crop

Crop is a designer's tool. Great identity is the result of taking away until only the essential remains.

The strategy read

A brand is strongest when it is reduced to its truth. We crop away the noise, the hedging and the inherited language until what is left is the one idea worth building on. Same instinct, both halves of the job.

How we work

Simple, fast and built to be used, not just launched.

It starts with questions. A lot of them. That is how we find out what makes you tick and where the real opportunity sits.

Discover

We immerse ourselves in your business, your market and your people. We read everything that matters and we run a focused workshop to surface the raw material.

Crop

We distil it. We find the organising idea and the brand on a page, the why, what and how, then sharpen it until it is undeniable.

Build

We bring it to life. Identity, voice and creative starters, made into a system you can actually run. We can keep going with the network from there.
